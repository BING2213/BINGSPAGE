Guernica

In the Film and Television advertising Class, I made the game "Guernica,"
which was an interactive PSA about waste sorting with 4 other students.
In the group, I was the director, programmer, animator, game designer, graphic designer, and so on
who finish most part of this game.
It won the silver prize of "The 5th Shanghai University Student Public Service Advertising Competition",
and be presented in "The 4th National College Students Network Culture Festival".
